# Privacy Policy — Nuri: Focus Friend

**Last updated: May 2026**

## Overview

Nuri - Focus Friend ("Nuri", "the extension", "we") is a Chrome browser extension built to help users focus by caring for a virtual pet. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data Nuri collects, how it is used, and what we never do.

---

## What data Nuri collects

Nuri collects and stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Browsing time data** — how many seconds you spend on focused vs. distraction websites each day
- **Focus streak** — how long your current uninterrupted focus session has been
- **Settings** — your chosen focus goal duration and break duration
- **Widget preferences** — whether the widget is visible or hidden

This data is stored using Chrome's `chrome.storage.local` API, which means it lives **only on your device** and is **never sent anywhere**.

---

## What Nuri does NOT collect

- Nuri does **not** collect your name, email address, or any personal information
- Nuri does **not** track or store the specific URLs or page content you visit
- Nuri does **not** send any data to external servers
- Nuri does **not** use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party services
- Nuri does **not** store any data in the cloud
- Nuri does **not** share any data with anyone

---

## How your data is used

Your locally stored data is used only to:

- Display your pet's mood based on your focus habits
- Show your daily focused and distracted time
- Trigger break reminders after your chosen focus goal

All processing happens entirely within your browser.

---

## Data retention

All data is stored locally and resets daily at midnight. You can manually reset your data at any time by clicking **"Reset Today"** in the extension popup. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data permanently.

---

## Permissions explained

Nuri requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `tabs` | To detect which website is active and whether it's a distraction site |
| `storage` | To save your focus stats and settings locally on your device |
| `alarms` | To keep the background tracker running reliably |

---

## Children's privacy

Nuri is designed for students and young adults aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
