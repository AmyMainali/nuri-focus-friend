### FULL DESCRIPTION

Meet Nuri — the tiny pixel pet who lives in your browser and cares about your focus.

Nuri is a productivity extension for students and young adults who want to build better browsing habits without strict blockers or boring timers. Instead of punishing you for getting distracted, Nuri creates a simple emotional connection: take care of your focus, and Nuri will thrive.

**How it works**
When you press START, Nuri begins watching your browsing. Spend time on focused work and Nuri grows happy and energetic. Spend too long on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Reddit, or Twitter, and Nuri becomes tired and sad. The choice is yours.

**Features**
- Live floating widget that sits on every page — always visible, never in the way
- 5 mood states: Happy, Good, Neutral, Tired, Sad — each with unique pixel art animations
- Real-time focus and distraction timer so you can see exactly how your day is going
- Break reminder — after your focus goal is reached, Nuri appears and asks you to rest
- Fully customizable — set your own focus goal and break duration
- Draggable widget — move it anywhere on the screen
- Start and stop tracking whenever you want — you are in full control
- Clean pixel art design with Pixelify Sans typography
- 100% private — all data stays on your device, nothing is ever sent anywhere

**Distraction sites tracked by default**
YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Reddit, X (Twitter), Netflix, Twitch

**Why Nuri?**
Most productivity tools feel like punishment. Nuri feels like responsibility. When you see your little pixel pet looking sad because you spent the last hour on social media, something clicks. Not because you were forced — because you care.

Focus better. Nuri is counting on you.
